This winter [Le Petit Vélo Rouge (LPVR)](<http://lepetitvelorouge.wordpress.com/>) has taken on the task of organizing our workshop series. All workshops will take place at the Flat, however a suggested pay-what-you-can donation of $5 towards starting up LPVR's new space is encouraged. Workshops are open to everyone. All workshops take place on Wednesdays from 7pm til 9pm. **January 11th: Bicycle Basics** – Fixing flat tires, regular bike maintenance, how to use tools; (Using wrenches, screwdrives, etc. so you don’t injure yourself or your tools!) **January 18th: Brakes** – Everything and anything to make sure you can stop! **January 25th: Ball Bearing systems** – Headsets, Hubs and 3-piece bottom brackets. **February 1st: Gears** – Getting your mechanical advantage just right! **February 8th: Wheels** – Truing, adjusting hubs, cleaning rims, building basics. **February 15th: Advanced topics** – Big tools; weird parts; compatibilities; the answer to life, theuniverse, and everything. How hammers will fix everything. **Please RSVP! Space is limited! internal.lpvr@gmail.com** [![](http://theflat.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/lpvrworkshops2012winter.jpg)](<http://theflat.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/lpvrworkshops2012winter.jpg>)
