Come help out at the Flat! If you've ever considered volunteering, Tasquerades are a great way to get more familiar with the space and meet some of the other volunteers. Every month, we give the shop a good clean-up and clear-out. There'll be food, drink, and music! Drop in any time between 2-5PM.

Il y a un tasquerade le prochain mercredi, le 15 Août. Allez aider le Flat ranger l'atelie et rencontre les autre bénévoles. Il y a de bouffe! Passez par le Flat entre 14 et 17h.
