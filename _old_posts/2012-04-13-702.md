[![](http://theflat.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/cns-gearswap-2012.jpg)](<http://theflat.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/cns-gearswap-2012.jpg>) Mark your calendars for the 3rd annual **Cyclo-Nord Sud Bicycle Gear-Swap** , April 28, 10am - 3pm. The swap has been a great place for cycling enthusiasts to meet and geek out, unload some of their extra items, and pick up some cool new stuff. Held at CNS's warehouse, **7235 St Urbain** (near Jean-Talon and Castelnau metro stations). If you plan to sell in volume, you can come an hour early to set up. Admission: $2 Sellers: $10 Cyclo Nord-Sud members: free More info or to book a table: recyclonordsud@gmail.com
