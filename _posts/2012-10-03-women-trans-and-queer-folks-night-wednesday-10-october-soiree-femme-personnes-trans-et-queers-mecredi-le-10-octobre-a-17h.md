Les soirées femme, personnes trans et queers sont le deuxième mercredi du mois. Au flat, on sait que tout le monde n'est pas confortables à l' atelier du vélo. Donc, on à une soirée au but d'aider des gens devenir plus confortable à l'atelier. Allez passer par le Flat **le 10 octobre à 17h** , et on vous enseigne comment réparer votre vélo. Our monthly WTQ is on Wednesday, the 10th of October at 5pm. At the Flat, we try to create a less oppressive space. We wish everyone was comfortable in a bike shop, but it just ain't true. If you are female, trans or queer and don't feel comfortable coming into the shop on regular nights, come by for our WTQF night. We'll show you how to fix your bike so that you feel more comfortable coming by during our regular open hours.
