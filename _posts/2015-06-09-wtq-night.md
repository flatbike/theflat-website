This Wednesday June 10, 2:30-5pm, is Women, Trans* and Queer Night at the Flat! On this one Wednesday a month, we open specially for self-identified women, trans* and/or queer folk to fix bikes with similarly identified volunteers in a supportive and non-judgmental environment.
